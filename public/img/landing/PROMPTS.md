# SmartWay landing — image prompts (English)

Place generated files here as:

- `hero.jpg` — first viewport (supermarket atmosphere)
- `aisle.jpg` — mid-scroll (aisles / wayfinding)
- `ops.jpg` — late scroll (operations / dashboard mood)

Recommended: 2400×1600 or 16:9, JPEG quality 80–85, no text/logos in the image.

---

and warm ceiling lights, shallow depth of field, elegant retail atmosphere,
no people, no logos, no text, cinematic composition, high-end RetailTech mood,
photorealistic, 16:9, negative space in the center for typography overlay
